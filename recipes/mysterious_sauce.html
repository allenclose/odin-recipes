Mysterious Sauce

Description

Nobody knows what this mysterious concoction does... The recipe was found in an ancient wizard tome. Does it even taste good?

Ingredients

  • Fairy Dust
  • Sweet Baby Ray's
  • Strange gooey substance commonly found under dank sinks
  • Four purple colored markers

Steps

  1. Using a mortar and pestle, grind the felt tips of the purple colored markers into a fine paste.
  2. Pour the fairy dust, Sweet Baby Ray's, strange gooey substance, and purple marker paste into a large bowl.
  3. Add a cup of water. Mix thoroughly, and serve...?
