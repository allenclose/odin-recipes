Terraforming Apple

Description

A Terraforming Apple- a must have in every household. Using such a powerful tool, you can telekinetically landscape and redecorate with the power of your mind (and apple) alone!

Ingredients

  • A ripe apple
  • Terraforming Matrix Plugin

Steps

  1. With one hand, grab the apple.
  2. With your other hand, place one end of the Terraforming Matrix Plugin against the apple. It should activate and automatically meld into the apple.
  3. And that's all there is to it! How simple was that?
